What it does

Six clinical workflow functions, fully automated.

Triage

Symptom assessment

Patients complete a guided symptom questionnaire before their visit. Responses are scored and flagged for the provider's review.

Intake

Digital intake forms

Insurance cards, medical history, consent forms collected digitally before arrival. No clipboards, no paper scanning.

Reminders

Appointment reminders

SMS and voice call reminders sent at configurable intervals (48h, 24h, 2h). Patients confirm or reschedule directly from the message.

No-show

No-show recovery

Patients who miss appointments receive an automated outreach sequence. The system identifies habitual no-shows and adjusts the reminder cadence.

Scheduling

Smart scheduling

Appointment slots recommended based on provider availability, patient preferences, and historical no-show risk for the time slot.

Compliance

HIPAA controls

Audit logging on every data access, BAA-ready deployment, PHI masking in error responses, signed URLs for sensitive documents.

Integrations

Connects to the systems your clinic already runs.

EMR

Electronic medical records

Connects to your EMR system via FHIR or HL7. Patient records stay in your system of record.

SMS/Voice

Appointment communications

Appointment reminders, no-show outreach, and confirmation flows via SMS and automated voice calls.

Billing

Insurance verification

Pre-visit insurance eligibility checks integrated into the intake flow. Flags coverage gaps before the patient arrives.

How it deploys

Live in 3 to 4 weeks. No infrastructure changes on your side.

Week 1-2

Integration and configuration

We connect to your EMR system, configure the form builder for your intake workflow, and set up the reminder schedule based on your appointment types.

Week 3

Shadow mode

CliniFlow runs alongside your front desk in shadow mode. Clinical staff review the triage scores, intake completions, and reminder sequences before anything goes live.

Week 4

Go live

Digital intake, appointment reminders, and no-show recovery go live. Your front desk shifts from manual data entry to exception handling. Audit logs active from day one.
