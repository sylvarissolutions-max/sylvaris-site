What it does

Six BPO functions, fully autonomous.

Sales

Outbound product sales

AI agents run outbound sales campaigns with scripted and adaptive call flows. Lead qualification, objection handling, and appointment setting at scale without hiring SDR teams.

Retention

Customer retention

Churn prediction triggers proactive retention calls. Win-back campaigns for lapsed customers. Renewal reminders and upsell offers delivered at the right moment in the customer lifecycle.

Service

Customer service

Inbound call handling, ticket triage, response drafting, and escalation routing. SLA compliance enforced automatically. Handles volume spikes without staffing changes.

Support

Technical support

L1 and L2 technical support powered by injected product knowledge bases. Troubleshooting flows, system diagnostics, and resolution tracking. Escalates to human specialists only when needed.

Analytics

Operations dashboard

Real-time visibility across all BPO functions: call volume, resolution rate, CSAT scores, agent utilization, SLA compliance, and revenue attribution. Exportable for client reporting.

Evolution

Continuous optimization

The system tracks which scripts, responses, and call flows produce the best outcomes and adjusts agent parameters over time. Performance improves without manual tuning.

Integrations

Connects to the tools your team already uses.

Helpdesk

Zendesk, Intercom, Freshdesk

Pulls tickets from your existing helpdesk via API. No migration required. BPO Commander reads from and writes back to your helpdesk system of record.

Comms

Slack and email

Escalation alerts and shift handoff summaries delivered to Slack channels. Email notifications for SLA breaches and high-priority tickets.

Voice

Telephony

Optional voice channel for outbound calls (payment reminders, appointment confirmations, follow-ups). Integrates with Twilio and compatible providers.

How it deploys

Live in 2 to 3 weeks. No infrastructure changes on your side.

Week 1

Discovery and integration

We connect to your helpdesk API, map your ticket categories, import your SLA definitions, and configure the routing rules based on your current escalation matrix.

Week 2

Shadow mode

BPO Commander runs alongside your existing team in shadow mode. It classifies and drafts but does not send. Your team reviews the output and provides feedback that tunes the system.

Week 3

Go live

Auto-send enabled on approved categories. Escalation routing takes over. Your team shifts from processing to reviewing. SLA compliance is tracked from day one.
