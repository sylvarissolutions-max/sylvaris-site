Privacy Policy

Effective April 11, 2026 Version 1.0

This Privacy Policy describes how Sylvaris Solutions LLC ("Sylvaris", "we", "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit our website, contact us, or enter into a commercial engagement. We keep this document short and written in plain language because enterprise buyers should not have to parse legalese to understand what we do with their data.

1. Who we are

Sylvaris Solutions LLC is a limited liability company that builds and operates production AI systems for enterprise operations. For questions about this policy or your data, contact sylvarissolutions@gmail.com.

2. What we collect

We collect only the information needed to respond to you and deliver services you have engaged us for.

When you visit the website

  • Standard server logs from our hosting provider, including IP address, browser type, referring URL, and timestamps
  • Basic analytics (page views, session duration) in aggregate form only

When you contact us

  • Your name, email address, company, and any details you include in your message
  • Correspondence history related to your inquiry

When you enter a commercial engagement

  • Contact details for your team members involved in the engagement
  • Billing and payment information as required to invoice and receive payment
  • Operational data you choose to share as part of building, integrating, or running a Sylvaris product on your behalf

3. How we use your information

  • To respond to you. When you contact us, we use your message to answer your question.
  • To deliver services. When you hire us, we use engagement data to build, integrate, test, operate, and support the Sylvaris product you have contracted for.
  • To meet legal and accounting obligations. Invoices, tax records, and contracts are retained for as long as applicable law requires.
  • To secure our systems. Logs are used to detect abuse, debug issues, and respond to security events.

We do not sell your information. We do not use your data to train AI models. We do not share your data with advertisers.

4. AI models and training data

Sylvaris products are built on third-party AI model APIs operated under enterprise terms that prohibit the use of customer inputs for model training. When you use a Sylvaris product, the prompts and responses generated for your workload are not used by us or by the underlying model provider to improve or retrain any model. This is also documented in our Data Processing Addendum.

5. Who we share information with

We share information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service providers. Cloud hosting, payment processing, email, and infrastructure providers who act as processors on our behalf under written contract.
  • Legal requirements. Where required by law, valid legal process, or to protect rights and safety.
  • Business transfers. In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may transfer to the successor entity under the terms of this policy.

A current list of our subprocessors is available upon request under NDA as part of a Data Processing Addendum.

6. International transfers

Sylvaris is based in the United States. If you are located outside the United States, your information may be transferred to, stored in, and processed in the United States. Where required, we rely on standard contractual clauses or equivalent legal mechanisms to provide an adequate level of protection for personal data.

7. Data retention

We retain contact inquiries for up to 24 months after the last communication. Commercial engagement data is retained for the duration of the engagement and for a reasonable period afterward as required for legal, accounting, and dispute-resolution purposes, and in accordance with the terms of the applicable services agreement.

8. Your rights

Depending on where you are located, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct information that is inaccurate or incomplete
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Request portability of your data
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

To exercise any of these rights, email sylvarissolutions@gmail.com. We respond to verified requests within 30 days.

9. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect information in our custody. No system is fully secure. In the event of a data incident affecting your information, we will notify affected customers and appropriate authorities as required by law and by the terms of the applicable Data Processing Addendum.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be announced on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
